# AI Lead Generation Agent 🤖🚀

An **AI-powered lead generation agent** that automates end-to-end prospect discovery, qualification, and pipeline creation.
The system analyzes business data, identifies high-fit prospects, and syncs qualified leads directly into a CRM for outreach.

This automation significantly reduces manual research and ensures sales teams focus only on high-quality, high-intent leads.

---

## 🔍 Overview

The AI Lead Generation Agent streamlines the entire prospecting workflow by:
- Discovering potential business leads automatically
- Analyzing company and contact data
- Qualifying leads using AI-based filtering
- Structuring the results into a clean lead pipeline
- Syncing qualified prospects into a CRM

Instead of manually researching companies and contacts, the agent performs automated market scanning and qualification, saving hours of manual work.
